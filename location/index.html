Location:

NETC 2017 will be hosted in historic Sacannah, Georgia. With its pedestrian-friendly layout and innovative urban design, Savannah serves as a balm for the senses. The city of Savannah inspires visitors with its emerald tree canopy, quaint cobblestone streets and majestic architecture. Take the time to wander off the beaten path and experience the history, beauty and charm of this beloved coastal city.

Hotel Information:

The conference will by held at the Hyatt Regency on the Savannah riverfront. More details will be released soon, but for more information on this hotel, visit the Hyatt's website.
