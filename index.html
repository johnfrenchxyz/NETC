Conference Information

Join us as the 2017 National Extension Technology Conference convenes in Savannah on June 13-15. Examine technological trends, share ideas and network with your peers while immersed in the natural beauty, charm, architecture and history of the South. Georgia’s First City will captivate, intrigue, and surprise you with her gracious hospitality and subtle eccentricities.

The NETC2017 Planning Committee is excited about the program that is coming together. Over the coming weeks and months, we will be sharing information about innovative program features and engagement tools to enhance your conference experience. Please stay tuned as our plans come together. We believe you will like what you see and hear!
